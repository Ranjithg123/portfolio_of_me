IoT Safety Helmet

The IoT Safety Helmet is an ESP32-based project designed for mining workers. It integrates sensors for gas detection, temperature, and humidity, along with an emergency alert system. Data is sent to an online dashboard in real time, enhancing workplace safety.

  • Built with ESP32, smoke sensor, temperature/humidity sensor.
  • Live monitoring via Adafruit IO dashboard.
  • Alerts through buzzer and LED when unsafe levels are detected.
  • Low-power design with rechargeable battery.
