IoT Safety Helmet

The IoT Safety Helmet is an intelligent, AI-powered device for ensuring worker safety in hazardous mining environments. It integrates ESP32 microcontroller, Firebase cloud database, and Google Gemini API for advanced analysis of sensor data.

  • ESP32 Data Acquisition: Collects real-time gas, temperature, and humidity readings inside the helmet.
  • Firebase Cloud Storage: Streams data securely for persistence and easy scalability.
  • Web Dashboard: Displays live and historical data with charts and safety indicators for supervisors.
  • Gemini AI Analysis: Detects abnormal gas levels, temperature spikes, and recommends safety actions.
  • Immediate alerts via buzzer and LEDs when hazardous conditions are detected.
  • Compact, rechargeable, and optimized for industrial field deployment.
